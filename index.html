Welcome to Buno Coffee!

Buno Coffee is the brainchild of Alireza, a 36-year-old passionate, perfectionist barista who has been in the coffee industry for 13 years. This place is run as a family collaboration and you will always be welcomed here like a friend. Here we are to brighten your day with a great cup of speciality coffee to start your day or chase away that afternoon slump!

About our products!

Have a sweet tooth but still don't want to miss out on the indulgence? Try our delicious homemade vegan sweets and still stay healthy. We have a wide selection of vegan products, which proves how serious we are about keeping our products healthy and natural. We always use freshly roasted specialty coffee beans to make the taste of your coffee as unique as possible. Here you can have a great selection of options for breakfast and lunch.
