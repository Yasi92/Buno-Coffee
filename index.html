Welcome to Buno Coffee!

Buno Coffee is the brainchild of Alireza, a 36-year-old passionate, perfectionist barista who has been in the coffee industry for 13 years. This place is run as a family collaboration and you will always be welcomed here like a friend. Here we are to brighten your day with a great cup of speciality coffee to start your day or chase away that afternoon slump!

About our products!

Looking for healthy sweets? Try our delicious homemade vegan sweets and stay healthy. We have a wide selection of vegan products next to using freshly roasted specialty coffee beans, which proves how serious we are about keeping our products healthy and high-quality. Here you will find a great healthy options for breakfast and lunch. Curious about what we have to offer? Take a look at our menu!

Our cozy place!

This is a great and cozy place to meet your loved ones and friends for a delicious meal or coffee. Wa also accept arrangements for special events. Get in touch for private events. Want to get some fresh air? Take a seat on our cozy street terrace and enjoy outside.
